ARMY COACHING

Bharat Academy: Your Destination for Army Physical Coaching

Welcome to Bharat Academy, where we specialize in comprehensive physical coaching designed specifically for aspirants preparing for a career in the Indian Army. We understand that physical fitness is crucial for success in Army selection processes. Our mission is to provide specialized training that helps you meet and exceed the physical requirements necessary for a successful career in the Armed Forces.

Why Choose Bharat Academy for Physical Coaching?

  • Expert Trainers: Our team of experienced trainers includes former defense personnel who bring valuable insights into the physical demands of Army training. They are dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals.

  • Specialized Training Modules: Our Army Physical Coaching focuses on essential physical skills, including:

    • Running:

      • Endurance runs to build stamina for the 1.6 km run.
      • Sprint training to enhance speed and explosive power.

    • Pull-Ups:

      • Techniques and strength training to improve your pull-up performance. We focus on building upper body strength and endurance through targeted exercises.

    • Long Jump:

      • Drills to enhance your jumping technique and distance, including plyometric exercises and practice jumps to ensure you meet the Army’s long jump requirements.

    • Sprints:

      • High-intensity sprint drills designed to increase speed and agility. This includes short-distance sprints to improve quickness and reaction time.

  • Mock Physical Tests: Regularly scheduled mock tests will simulate actual Army physical fitness assessments. This includes timed runs, pull-up tests, long jump practice, and sprint evaluations to gauge your readiness.

  • Progress Tracking and Feedback: Our trainers continuously monitor your progress and provide constructive feedback. Regular assessments keep you motivated and on track to achieve your fitness goals.

  • Supportive Training Environment: At Bharat Academy, you’ll train in a positive and motivating atmosphere, surrounded by peers who share your aspirations. Our community encourages you to push your limits and succeed together.

Admission Process

Starting your journey with our Army Physical Coaching is simple:

  1. Registration: Fill out our online registration form to secure your spot in our training program.
  2. Initial Assessment: Attend an introductory session to evaluate your current fitness level and establish your training goals.
  3. Enrollment: Choose your training program and complete the enrollment process.
